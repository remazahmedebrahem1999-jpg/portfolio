# Projects

## Project 1 – Technical Communication Fundamentals
This project focused on understanding the basics of technical communication, including how to convey information clearly and effectively. It addressed communication barriers and strategies to overcome them, helping me develop skills in explaining technical concepts.

## Project 2 – Audience Awareness Skills
In this project, I learned how to adapt messages depending on the audience. Understanding audience needs, expectations, and prior knowledge helped me tailor content to be clear, engaging, and appropriate for different groups.

## Project 3 – Router Fundamentals
This project explained routers in simple and technical terms. It covered the purpose of routers in networking, how they direct data traffic, and their importance in connecting devices to the internet efficiently and securely.

## Project 4 – Personal Strengths and Weaknesses
This project involved self-reflection on my personal strengths and weaknesses. I identified my key strengths such as problem-solving, adaptability, and communication skills, and noted areas for improvement including creativity, persuasion, and managing emotions.
